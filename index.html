Tips for Success

Following a long-term plan can be tough. Keep these tips in mind to stay motivated, avoid burnout, and make the most of your study time.

1. Be Flexible

This plan is a guide, not a prison. If you're tired, a 1-hour session is better than zero. If a topic is easy, move on. If it's hard, spend more time.

2. Active Learning

Don't just watch videos or read. Code the algorithms. Draw the system designs. Explain concepts out loud.

3. Protect Your Time

Your 8-10 PM 'me time' is critical. Don't skip it. Burnout is the enemy.

4. Track Your Progress

Use a simple notebook or Trello board. It feels good to check things off.

5. Active Job Applications

The 2 hours on Saturday are for *active* work: tailoring your resume, writing cover letters, and networking on LinkedIn. Don't just passively scroll.

6. Weekly Review

The 1-hour Sunday review is your steering wheel. Use it to adjust the plan based on what you've learned and where you're struggling.
